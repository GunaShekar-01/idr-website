About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on preparing professionals for digital, cyber and technology risk roles. IDR bridges the gap between academic research and real-world implementation.

Through collaboration with universities and industry partners, IDR helps develop skilled practitioners capable of managing risk in highly regulated and high-consequence environments.

Our Model

Academy

Training and bootcamps for students and professionals entering digital risk careers.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing intellectual property, AI governance models and future risk frameworks.

Advisory Services

Helping organisations implement security and governance frameworks such as ISO 27001 and NIST.

Our Community

IDR supports students, early-career professionals and experienced practitioners working in financial services, technology and critical infrastructure sectors. Our programs focus on building the next generation of digital risk specialists.
